Rules

There is one fake English word and two real English words.

You need to find the fake one as fast as possible.

Choose your difficulty and start playing!

Leaderboard

Score Calculation: (Difficulty * Score) / Duration

Difficulty Multipliers: Hard = 1.5, Normal = 1, Easy = 0.5
